This week’s 5-minute reset

Step 1 — Downshift (60s)

Sit. Feet on the floor. Exhale longer than you inhale for 6 slow breaths.

Step 2 — Unload (2 min)

Write 3 bullets:

  • What’s still “open” in my mind
  • What can wait until tomorrow
  • One next action (tiny)

Step 3 — Close the day (2 min)

Choose one closure cue: tidy one surface, set out tomorrow’s first item, or dim lights.
